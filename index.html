How to Install APK Files

Follow these simple steps to install APK files on your Android device

Enable Unknown Sources

Go to Settings > Security and enable "Install from unknown sources" to allow APK installation.

Download the APK

Click the download button for your desired version. The APK will be saved to your Downloads folder.

Install the App

Open the downloaded APK file and tap "Install". Wait for the installation to complete.

Launch & Enjoy

Once installed, open the app from your app drawer. You're all set!
